Meet The Founder

Gage Terrazas

University of Texas Graduate · Founder & Operator

I'm a University of Texas graduate with a degree in Communication and a minor in Entrepreneurship, and the founder of TRZ Shine & Detail. With over five years of hands-on experience in the detailing industry, my background began at a private boat club, where I refined my craft servicing high-end vessels and developed a meticulous standard for quality and care.

Over the past year, I've brought that same level of precision into the automotive space, building a brand centered around excellence, discretion, and consistency. I am also proud to provide detailing services at Barton Creek Country Club, serving members, their guests, and resort guests with the same premium, white-glove experience.

Every vehicle I work on is treated with a tailored approach, using professional-grade products and proven techniques to deliver a true showroom-level finish. My goal is simple — deliver an exceptional, reliable service that clients can trust and take pride in.
